About Me

Hi, I’m Davud Bunyatov, also known as dbun5327 on social media. I’ve been dedicated to improving my physique and building my personal brand for the past four years. Originally from Baku, Azerbaijan, I’ve lived in Glasgow, Scotland since I was a kid. Bodybuilding has always been a passion of mine, and I’m currently working toward competing naturally in 2026. Over the years, I’ve coached several friends as part of my personal training journey, and now I’m focused on expanding my business, connecting with new people, and helping others transform their bodies and confidence.

My mission is to help people lose fat and gain muscle so they can overcome insecurities and feel proud of their physiques—all done naturally, with zero PEDs. I believe in a simple, old-school training approach combined with proven science, a healthy diet, and of course, the occasional off meal. My coaching is based on smart, evidence-based training and nutrition, customized to fit each person’s goals, lifestyle, and body type.

If you're ready to take your fitness journey seriously, let’s work together and start building your dream physique today. I’ve already helped several people get leaner, stronger, and more confident—check out their results in the testimonial section below.

In addition to coaching, I’m a student at the University of Glasgow, studying Aerospace Engineering. I’ve been consistently sharing my fitness journey and content across social media for the past 2–3 years—follow me at @dbun5327 on TikTok and Instagram to stay up to date with training tips, motivation, and behind-the-scenes of my own progress.
